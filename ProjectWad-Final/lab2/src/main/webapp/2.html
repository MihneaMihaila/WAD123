Negoiu (M. Făgăraşului), 2535 m

Negoiu Peak is a mountain peak in the Făgăraș Mountains of the Southern Carpathians, being located in Sibiu County, Romania, with an elevation of 2,535 metres (8,317 ft).

It is the second highest peak in Romania after Moldoveanu Peak (2544m).
