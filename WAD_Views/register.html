About Hikey

Hikey is a web application targeted for hiking enthusiasts and also people that want to get into it. Experienced hikers will post recommendations of places to go to, ratings of places, and even post pictures of the place, with observations. Getting into hiking can feel overwhelming, especially if you don't know people that already do it. So we are building a community that will solve the anxiety around that.
